Community Service & Social Responsibility

Over 50 VIA Hours - Making a Difference

Committed to community service through various volunteering initiatives, accumulating over 50 VIA (Values in Action) hours. Through volunteering activities and elderly engagement programmes, I learned the importance of empathy, service, and community involvement.
