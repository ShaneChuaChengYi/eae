Blox Fruit Business Analytics

Business Model Overview

Through strategic trading on Carousell, I developed a sustainable business model by purchasing Blox Fruit game passes, trading them for rare in-game fruits, and reselling them at a premium. This taught me key entrepreneurial lessons in inventory management, market dynamics, pricing strategy, and profit optimization. I found a dealer on carousel which sold me 1Krobux (in game currency)/ S$5 Which i used to buy the gamepasses and trade them for "fruits" in the game which i would find buyers for, making a profit from it.
